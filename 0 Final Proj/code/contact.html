Helplines

Helplines for anxiety serve as lifelines of support and understanding for individuals navigating the challenges of this common mental health concern. These dedicated services provide a safe and confidential space where individuals can express their feelings, fears, and uncertainties without judgment.

Website Hotline Services
www.suicidepreventionlifeline.org 1-800-273-8255 (USA) 24/7 crisis support and prevention for individuals in distress, including those with anxiety
www.samaritans.org 116 123 (UK and Ireland) 24/7 emotional support for anyone experiencing feelings of distress or anxiety
www.beyondblue.org.au 1300 22 4636 (Australia) Information, support, and resources for anxiety and depression-related concerns
www.crisisservicescanada.ca 1-833-456-4566 (Canada) 24/7 crisis support for individuals experiencing anxiety or any emotional distress
www.lifeline.org.nz 0800 543 354 (New Zealand) 24/7 helpline providing emotional support for individuals experiencing anxiety or any crisis
www.befrienders.org.my 03-7956 8145 (Malaysia) 24/7 helpline providing emotional support and crisis intervention for individuals experiencing feelings of distress or loneliness

Join our Volunteer Group

Are you passionate about mental health and committed to making a positive impact on those struggling with anxiety? Join the Calm Mind volunteer team and be part of a supportive community dedicated to helping others find peace and resilience. As a volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to contribute your unique skills in areas like content creation, community outreach, or program support. Whether you're an aspiring writer, a social media whiz, or an empathetic listener, your involvement will make a meaningful difference in the lives of those seeking anxiety relief. Together, let's create a nurturing space where individuals can find solace and strength on their journey to a calmer mind. Join us today and be a beacon of hope for those in need.
